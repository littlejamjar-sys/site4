What's on offer

Services

Camper Window Installation

Professional cutting and fitting of campervan windows. I use the right tools for a clean, accurate cut and ensure every window is properly sealed and finished — no rattles, no leaks. Suitable for all common van types including VW T5/T6, Transit, Sprinter and more.

Roof Vent Installation

Supply and installation of ventilation units including MaxxAir fans and Fiamma vents. Cutting the roof of your van is one of the most nerve-wracking jobs — getting it done by someone experienced makes a big difference. All installs are fully waterproofed and tested.

Insulation & Wall Prep

Help preparing your van interior ready for a full camper conversion. Good insulation makes a massive difference to comfort, condensation and noise. I can advise on materials and methods, or help fit whatever approach suits your budget and van.

Interior Build Help

Hands-on guidance and labour for cabinetry, bed platforms, storage layouts and interior build stages. Whether you need help with the whole fit-out or just one tricky section, I can work alongside you or take on specified parts of the build.

Conversion Consultation

Planning to convert your van but not sure where to start? I offer practical advice on planning your layout, choosing materials, estimating costs and working through the conversion stages in the right order — based on real experience building and living in a van.

Mobile Service Available

I come to you.

Where possible, all work can be carried out at your location using a fully equipped service van. No need to drag your van to a workshop — I'll sort it on your driveway, at your campsite, or wherever suits you.
